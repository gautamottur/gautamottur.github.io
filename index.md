# Gautam Ottur

Hi there! 👋 My name is Gautam Ottur. I'm a linguist who is interested in the modelling of the relationships between form and meaning, based on how these relationships vary. I defended my PhD at the Georg-August Universität Göttingen under [Hedde Zeijlstra](https://www.heddezeijlstra.org/) and [Markus Steinbach](http://www.markus-steinbach.de/) in late 2025, and am currently working as a research associate at the University of Kassel. On this website, you can find information about me and my work.
